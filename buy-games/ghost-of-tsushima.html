The 13th century is coming to an end, and the Mongol Empire has crushed entire nations in its attempt to conquer the East. Mud, blood and steel: Give your enemies a taste of your samurai sword in nerve-wracking skirmishes, learn the art of the bow to take out threats from afar, and sneak up on enemies to eliminate them with surprising attacks.
